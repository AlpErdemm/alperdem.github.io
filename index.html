Highschool and Early Education

Being son of a doctor and a teacher, my childhood was spent in different cities attending the highest-end schools, getting the best education possible while playing sports in school team.

University

Computer Engineering was my dream throughout my highscool years, motivating me to study for the university entrance exam. It happened, I received chance to study at one of the best Computer Engineering faculties, at Gebze Technical University. Being the best also meant that it was the hardest, thus my college years were filled with study sessions and performing projects. I was a part of the game development club, tutoring friends and a player at college tennis club.

First commercial product

Fight and Seek is a mobile game available on Google Play, strategy category. It is the first end-to-end project I created and published for public use. Various struggles like game design, asset creation, music & sound effects, testing, team management, monetization and localization had been a great experience.

Employment - Faradai.ai

Faradai is a UK-based software firm that develops products to help organisations track, report, and reduce emissions using AI with 200+ international enterprise clients, including brands like IKEA, Marks & Spencer, Heineken, Shell, Sephora, BNP Paribas, Gap, Banana Republic, ISS, Vodafone, and PepsiCo. All of this is achieved by an underlying web application structure (server-client) and I worked as a Java Developer on the Server-Side for 2+ years.

Second commercial product

Colorfall is a mobile game available on both Google Play and App Store, casual category. My goal after creating a strategy game (FnS) was to reach more people and implement a simple and fun gameplay.
